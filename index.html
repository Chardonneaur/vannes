Why Choose Our Program?

The Master in Digital Marketing program at the University of Bretagne Sud is meticulously designed to equip students with the latest skills and knowledge needed to excel in the ever-evolving field of digital marketing. Situated in the picturesque city of Vannes, the program offers a unique blend of theoretical learning and practical application through our innovative alternance (work-study) format.

Students benefit from a curriculum that is regularly updated to reflect the latest trends and technologies in digital marketing. Our program emphasizes hands-on learning, providing students with real-world projects and internships that enhance their understanding and prepare them for successful careers.

Course Details

Our Master in Digital Marketing program spans over two years and includes a variety of modules designed to provide a deep understanding of digital marketing principles and practices.

Year 1: Students will cover the foundations of digital marketing, including:

  • Introduction to Digital Marketing
  • Web Analytics and Data Interpretation
  • Social Media Strategies
  • Content Marketing and Management
  • SEO (Search Engine Optimization) Fundamentals

Year 2: The second year delves into advanced topics and includes:

  • Advanced SEO Techniques
  • Digital Advertising and PPC (Pay-Per-Click)
  • Data-Driven Marketing Strategies
  • Capstone Project
  • Professional Internship
